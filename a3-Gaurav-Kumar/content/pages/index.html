Phantom Lake

From Wikipedia: Phantom Lake

Phantom Lake is a remote lake located in the province of British Columbia, Canada. Known for its clear waters and peaceful surroundings, it’s a perfect getaway for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. The lake is surrounded by forested areas that are rich with wildlife, making it a popular spot for camping and hiking.

Okanagan Lake

From Wikipedia: Okanagan Lake

Okanagan Lake (Okanagan: kłúsxnítkw) is a lake in the Okanagan Valley of British Columbia, Canada. The lake is 135 km (84 mi) long, between 4 and 5 km (2.5 and 3.1 mi) wide, and has a surface area of 348 km² (135 sq. mi.). Okanagan Lake is a fjord lake as it has been carved out by repeated glaciations. It is not uncommon for the lake to be 100 meters (328 ft) deep only 10 meters (11 yd) offshore.

Cultus Lake

From Wikipedia: Cultus Lake

Cultus Lake is a popular recreational destination located near Chilliwack, British Columbia, Canada. Known for its warm water and sandy beaches, it attracts visitors for swimming, boating, and fishing. Surrounded by mountains and forest, Cultus Lake is part of the Fraser Valley Regional District and offers various outdoor activities, making it an ideal spot for families and nature enthusiasts.
